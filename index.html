About ViBha Rajasthan

Vigyan Bharati, popularly known as VIBHA has its roots in Rajasthan as well. As per provisions of Vibha, Rajasthan is divided geographically in three divisions, called as Prants. These prants are Jaipur, Chittor and Jodhpur. The activities of VIBHA are being held in Kota, Ajmer and Jaipur.

Jaipur Prant

VIBHA Jaipur prant is registered with Company of Registrar. This Unit has been working since the year 2002 with an active founding participation of Dr. R. K. Bansal and his team. This unit has been organizing various activities to fulfil the aim of Vijnana Bharati.
