Zones and Regions

The complete nation will be divided six geographical zones. The states lying in each zone would be further divided into various regions. For example, Rajasthan has seven regions:

  1. Ajmer Division
  2. Bharatpur Division
  3. Bikaner Division
  4. Jaipur Division
  5. Jodhpur Division
  6. Kota Division
  7. Udaipur Division
