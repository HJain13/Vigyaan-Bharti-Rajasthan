IISF 2018

Lucknow

Students Engineering Models Competition

Engineering and Professional Students will present their innovative and creative projects, and the awards will be presented. Mega Organisations like ISRO, DRDO, BARC etc. will visit this Expo and the best projects would be adopted for their Organisations

  • About the Competition
  • Zones and Regions

About the Competiton

There will be 4 categories according to the academic level of students.

  1. Research scholars (PhD. Students)
    1. Technology(Engineering)
    2. Science
    3. Agriculture
    4. Biotechnology
  2. P.G Students
    1. Technology (Engineering)
    2. Science
    3. Agriculture
    4. Biotechnology
  3. Graduate students
    1. Technology (Engineering)
    2. Science
    3. Agriculture
    4. Biotechnology
  4. Polytechnic (Engineering)
    1. Technology (Engineering)
  5. Grass root Innovators

Zones and Regions

The complete nation will be divided six geographical zones. The states lying in each zone would be further divided into various regions. For example, Rajasthan has seven regions:

  1. Ajmer Division
  2. Bharatpur Division
  3. Bikaner Division
  4. Jaipur Division
  5. Jodhpur Division
  6. Kota Division
  7. Udaipur Division

Procedure

Synopsis of projects will have to be submitted in the website and only selected projects will be allowed to participate in the division level competition.

Selection Of projects for national level competition will be accomplished in two steps.

  1. District Level competition
  2. State level competition

In the First step, District level Competition will be conducted in each district Of Rajasthan.
There Will be 10 categories of students to be registered to participate in the competition.

  1. Ph.D. and PG Students of science, Agriculture, Biotechnology, and engineering.
  2. Graduate Students (Electronics, Communication, IOT, CS, IT)
  3. Graduate Students (Electrical, Automation, Robotics)
  4. Graduate Students (Mechanical, Automobile, Civil)
  5. Graduate Students (Physics, Chemistry, Agriculture, Biotechnology)
  6. Polytechnic /ITI students (All Streams) School Students(11th, 12th science Stream)
  7. Aeromodelling (Any Students)
  8. Projects Using Waste Material (Any student)
  9. Environment Sustainability (Any student)

Two Awards (First and second Will Be Judged By Experts in accordance with the parameters set by Vigyan Bharti)

Maximum 20 students from each district will be selected to participate in state-level competition.

(Total 20 winners in all categories) Top 2 District Winners (in each category) will be provided with • Certificate • Memento • Entry in IISF 2018 state level competition. (Total 20 winners in all categories) In competition at any level, If total entries (presented during event) in any category are less than 5 than projects of that categories may not be included in judgment process, subject to decision of juries.
